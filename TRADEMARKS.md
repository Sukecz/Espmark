# Espmark Trademark And Brand Policy

Espmark is the name of an independent benchmark project for ESP8266 and
ESP32-family boards.

The Espmark name, logo, icon, visual identity, website design, official
leaderboard, result database, and related brand assets are reserved by the
Espmark project owner.

## Allowed Use

You may use the Espmark name to:

- refer to the Espmark project,
- link to the official Espmark website or GitHub repository,
- describe compatibility with Espmark benchmark results,
- discuss, review, or compare Espmark results,
- publish benchmark results generated by official Espmark firmware.

Examples of acceptable use:

- "Tested with Espmark"
- "Compatible with Espmark result JSON"
- "Unofficial analysis of Espmark benchmark results"
- "Espmark score for my ESP32-C6 board"

## Not Allowed Without Permission

You may not use the Espmark name, logo, or visual identity to:

- claim that your project is the official Espmark project,
- claim endorsement, sponsorship, certification, or partnership,
- create a confusingly similar website, leaderboard, logo, or benchmark service,
- sell products or services under the Espmark name,
- use Espmark branding in advertising,
- modify Espmark results or scoring rules and present them as official results.

## Forks And Modified Versions

Forks and modified versions are allowed under the software license, but they
must clearly state that they are unofficial.

Modified versions must not use the Espmark name or logo in a way that could
confuse users into believing they are the official Espmark project.

Suggested wording for forks:

> This is an unofficial fork of Espmark and is not affiliated with or endorsed
> by the official Espmark project.

## Official Website And Leaderboard

Only results submitted through the official Espmark website, using official
Espmark firmware and official scoring rules, may be presented as official
Espmark results.

Unofficial tools may parse or display Espmark-compatible result files, but they
must not claim to be the official Espmark leaderboard.

## Manufacturer Names

Board names, manufacturer names, product names, and silicon family names are
used only to identify hardware and improve result filtering. Those names may be
trademarks of their respective owners.

Their presence in the board catalog, result pages, firmware metadata, or
documentation does not imply affiliation, endorsement, sponsorship,
certification, or partnership.

## Partner And Sponsor Use

Manufacturers, sponsors, partners, stores, and other commercial entities may
use Espmark branding only with prior written permission.

Partner badges, sponsor logos, official test labels, certification claims, and
promotional use require approval from the Espmark project owner.

Sponsorship or partnership does not influence benchmark scoring, methodology,
or published results.

## Contact

For permission to use the Espmark name, logo, or brand assets, contact the
Espmark project owner through the official GitHub repository or official
Espmark website.
